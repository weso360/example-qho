Guitar Amps, Effects & Monitor Speaker Repairs & Servicing

We have been providing a high quality service, repairing and servicing guitar amps for many years. Working on custom and branded equipment such as Marshall, Fender, Peavey and Orange, as well as more obscure brands such as WEM. Whether you have an older amp you want restoring / servicing, or something a little more modern, call us, or drop by the workshop to speak with an engineer. We also have experience on repairing speakers that may have taken a little punishment from being thrown into the back of the van..
  • Specialist repairs to Vintage and modern Amplifiers (Valve and Solid state)
  • Total Amp re-valves to individual blown valves identified / replaced (singles, matched pairs & matched quads) Amp rebiasing
  • Tube bases repaired & replaced
  • PM Guitar Tubes - Pre-Amp inc High gain & Low microphony, Power & Rectifier tubes
  • Unbalanced and noisy channels serviced
  • Faulty posts, switches, jacks, broken connections
  • Suspect connections and faulty crossovers
